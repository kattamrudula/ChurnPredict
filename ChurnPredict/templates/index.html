Channel Selection & Configuration

Connect ChurnPredict to diverse feedback sources like social media and forms. Easily define keywords for data collection and set flexible daily or weekly schedules. Get precise control over your incoming customer insights.

Feedback Normalization

Transform messy, unstructured feedback (text/images) into clean, actionable data. Map source columns to domain-specific targets using direct, banding, or Gemini-powered "Prompt" scoring. Prepare your data for powerful predictions.

Churn Prediction

Leverage our rich, normalized data to train a Logistic Regression machine learning model. This model proactively forecasts customer churn likelihood. Gain vital insights to identify and target at-risk customers early.

Actionable Insights Dashboard

Visualize churn predictions, present churn rates, and key statistical insights in one intuitive dashboard. Understand customer behavior and trends at a glance. Make data-driven decisions to boost retention and protect your bottom line.
